ABOUT TRCF

THE REDEEMED CHRISTIAN FELLOWSHIP or TRCF is an evangelical church community located at F. Torres St. Davao City (8000) founded by Rev. Dinah Edejer.

Rooted in discipleship teachings, for over 35 years, the Lord has used TRCF to bless people—fulfilling His agenda; “None shall perish,” through continuously spreading God’s love and word to the community.

For decades, TRCF has become more than just a church, it became a family—a home to many.

Our Mission

To reach the lost, make disciples, and build generations rooted in Christ’s love.

Our Vision

To see every family transformed by the power of the Gospel through intentional discipleship.

Our Core Values

Faith, Love, Unity, Integrity, and Servant Leadership are the heart of who we are.
